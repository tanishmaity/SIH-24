The Bhubneshwari Temple

The Bhubneshwari Temple is a prominent religious site located in the Dumka district of Jharkhand, India. Situated atop a hill, the temple is dedicated to Goddess Bhubneshwari, a form of Shakti (the Divine Mother) in Hinduism. The temple holds immense cultural, historical, and religious significance for the region, drawing devotees and visitors throughout the year.

Due to its location in a relatively remote part of Jharkhand, the Bhubneshwari Temple is not as easily accessible as other popular temples in India. However, it has become increasingly recognized as a site of religious tourism. Visitors can reach the temple by road, with Dumka being the nearest town. Public and private transportation options are available to reach the temple from major towns and cities in Jharkhand.
