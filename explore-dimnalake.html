Dimna Lake

Dimna Lake is an artificial reservoir located near Dalma Wildlife Sanctuary, 15 km from the city of Jamshedpur in the state of Jharkhand. Constructed in 1944, by Tata Steel to deal with the water scarcity in the city.The lake is part of the eco-tourism initiatives promoted by the local administration. It is maintained as a clean and serene environment, attracting visitors who appreciate nature. Local efforts are made to preserve its surroundings to maintain the ecological balance.

The best time to visit Dimna Lake is during the winter months (November to February), when the weather is pleasant, and the greenery is at its peak. The monsoon season (June to September) also enhances the beauty of the lake as the hills around it become lush and the lake fills up with water. It is a favored destination for picnics and outings. Visitors often come here for activities such as boating, kayaking, and swimming. Rowboats and paddle boats are available for rent, making it a great place for family outings.Dimna Lake, with its serene atmosphere and scenic surroundings, is one of the most peaceful and popular spots in the Jamshedpur area. It’s an ideal place for those seeking relaxation amidst nature or wanting to engage in light adventure activities.
