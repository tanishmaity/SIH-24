Handia

Handia (also spelled as Hadia) is a traditional rice beer made by the indigenous tribal communities in Jharkhand, including those living in and around Jamshedpur. It holds significant cultural importance in the region and is often consumed during social gatherings, festivals, and rituals. Handia is not only a beverage but a symbol of the rich heritage of the tribal people of Jharkhand.
